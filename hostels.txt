                                                                     UNIVERSITY OF PETROLEUM AND ENERGY STUDIES

                                                                                   HOSTELS
University of Petroleum and Energy Studies has separate hostel for boys and girls with a capacity of more than 2000 students at Bidholi and Kandoli campuses. 
All the hostels have wi fi facility for learning.Common room has been provided in each hostel for relaxation and 24*7 power back up and solar power water heaters facility is available.
The hostels have mess facility.Air conditioned hostels are also available. 
UPES has also tied up with private hostels for girls students in the city to offer appropriate accommodation outside the campus. All accommodation is on a triple sharing basis.
